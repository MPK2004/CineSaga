About Us

**VCET Diaries** is the official media and creative team of Vivekananda College of Engineering and Technology (VCET). We are a passionate group of students dedicated to capturing the moments, memories, and milestones that define campus life. From photography and videography to design and content creation, our team works to showcase the vibrant spirit and achievements of our college community. We document events, highlight student talent, and bring stories to life through our lenses and creativity. More than just a media team, VCET Diaries is a family of innovators who blend technology with artistry to preserve and promote the essence of VCET.
